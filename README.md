# 🐾 Sonic Palette: Meow Edition

**A browser-based audio puzzle game. Can you tune a cat?**

## 🎮 Play Now

👉 **[https://gamtruliar.github.io/WaveMeow/](https://gamtruliar.github.io/WaveMeow/)**

No install. No sign-up. Just open and play.

---

## How to Play

1. The game loads a default meow sound (or upload your own).
2. Hit **Start Game** — listen carefully for **5 seconds**.
3. Use the three sliders to recreate what you heard:
   - **Pitch** — how high or low the cat sounds
   - **Timbre** — chunky senior cat vs. tiny kitten resonance
   - **Echo** — dry close-up vs. reverb-soaked valley
4. Hit **Confirm** when you think you've matched it.
5. Complete **5 rounds** and see your final score.

## Scoring

Each slider is scored by how close you got to the hidden target value.  
Perfect match = **100%** per parameter. Final score is the average across all three and all five rounds.

| Score | Rank |
|-------|------|
| 95%+ | Tetrachromat of Sound 🎯 |
| 85%+ | Cat Whisperer 😼 |
| 70%+ | Pretty good ears 🎧 |
| 50%+ | Decent. Keep practicing 🐱 |
| 30%+ | Are you sure you're listening? 😐 |
| <30% | Go wash your ears 🚿 |
